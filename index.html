The Glendale Chamber of Congress Mission

Our mission is to help businesses in the city foster a strong network and help make connections. This chamber is focused on helping to strengthen the business interests of the city to help improve the well-being of all its citizens. This is a place where new, old, large or small businesses can join and get more advertisement to improve their business.

Being a Member means

  • Being connected with the community
  • Having access to more opportunites that help advertise my company
  • Having access to programs & services to improve my financial success
  • Taking advantage of dependable sources for community information
  • Greater opportunity to create contacts for business referrals
